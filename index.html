Younes's Portfolio (Freshman Year)

Here's a collection of my best work I have completed throughout my Freshman Year at MCST!

hack.halloween

The image you see above is my hack.halloween project. It was made in the Processing IDE for Windows using Java. It is a game in which the player is a pumpkin, and the objective is to dodge flying Party City plastic knives. There's a score counter at the top left, and the longer you stay alive, the higher your score gets. You lose if you are hit by a knife.

DesignQuest

This is my part in DesignQuest, and I'm proud of my game. Our DesignQuest objective was to make educational games that teach kids math. I made a multiplication game with fish in which the user multiplies one side of the screen's fish by the other side. The user does the math, types their answer in the box, and clicks the button to check the answer. If they are right, they will get 1 point added, and if they're wrong, they don't get any points. There are a total of 20 questions.

Creature Lab

This image is a class project. It's a simulation of a landscape with creatures constructed by students in my class. Everyone used classes to give their sprites functionality, and noise() was incorporated in their classes as well in some way. I worked on the mooshroom cows (red and white spotted) and I believe they turned out great! The sprite has great detail for being such a low resolution (32x32) and I used noise to control the random, fluid movements of the cow.

Brick Breaker

The image you see is my Brick Breaker project. The player controls a platform with their mouse, and the goal is to hit the beige block while not allowing the ball to fall into the void below. If the brick is hit, it will respawn somewhere random in the top third of the map and the player will be awarded with 1 point.

CIS Game Jam

This here is the Game Jam I worked on with Gabriel Arias and Christian Haase. It is a FNAF-style game which was built with the "Expect the Unexpected" theme. In the game, you are in an office. You have a taser tool (only works on Yo) and you have a computer screen, which allows you access to the cameras. If Gurt shows up, you must turn on the lights and wait for him to leave, and if Yo shows up, you have to close the curtain and wait for him to leave. Yo can jumpscare you, and you have to tase at the right moment in order to avoid it. The way you win is to survive until 6AM.
